Top three activies to do in New York City

Central Park

Central Park is America's first public landscaped park, covering 843 acres of land in the middle of NYC.

The National 9/11 Memorial & Museum

The National September 11 Memorial & Museum, a nonprofit in New York City, remembers and honors the 2,983 people killed in the horrific attacks of September 11, 2001.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

The Met is the museum to discover endless pieces of art covering over 2,000 years plus artifacts that are much older.
